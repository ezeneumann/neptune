Emerging Themes & Trends

 

  • Value-Plus Offerings: Despite macro caution, consumers and B2B buyers are considering higher-value propositions that solve for friction, save time, or align with evolving values (e.g., sustainability).
  • Digital Experience Acceleration: Investments in digital interfaces, live commerce, and hybrid service models are creating new expectations for omnichannel interaction and seamlessness.
  • Innovation Risk-Taking in Mature Categories: Brands under pressure from stagnation or contraction, like cereals, are increasingly deploying seasonal, limited-time and format-driven experiments supported by rapid behavioral testing.
  • AI-Enabled Customer Experience: Pilots of generative AI solutions are extending from B2C travel into B2B, reframing “service excellence” around automation and personalization.
  • Return to Core, with a Twist: There’s movement towards core product lines refreshed with incremental innovation—such as new packaging, flavor variants, or convenience-centric features—rather than breakthrough disruptions.
