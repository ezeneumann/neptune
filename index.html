Emerging Themes & Trends

 

  • Nostalgia and “newstalgia”: Brands are leveraging familiar formats with modern twists (e.g., retro cereals with functional claims), effectively activating emotional connections as consumers seek comfort and optimism.
  • Value-seeking and premium trade-ups coexist: Selective premiumization (e.g., Delta cabin upgrades) is thriving even as overall value sensitivity remains elevated. Consumers are willing to pay more for perceived quality, safety, or digital convenience.
  • AI-enabled customer experience: Investments in AI-powered personalization and content moderation (notably by Google, YouTube) are accelerating, as brands aim to improve trust and engagement while maintaining efficiency.
  • Workplace well-being and hybrid productivity: B2B buyers are assigning greater weight to solutions that support flexible work and employee well-being, shaping purchase criteria across office supplies and hygiene categories.
  • Category convergence: Cross-category product pairings and expanded bundling (e.g., batteries and outdoor gear) are resurfacing as brands look for incremental revenue streams and new usage occasions.
