Emerging Themes & Trends

 

  • AI-Enabled Personalization: Both B2C and B2B brands accelerating investments in next-gen, AI-powered customer journeys, driven by higher expectations for tailored engagement and service efficiency.
  • Convenience Remains King: Frictionless experiences—spanning app-based ordering to rapid digital onboarding—are capturing incremental growth and driving user retention, especially as time-starved consumers remain “trade-up” selective.
  • Premiumization Amid Value Concerns: Even as some spending softens, brands offering clear value upgrades (premium features, ingredient quality, exclusive experiences) are seeing mid-single-digit growth versus mainstream counterparts.
  • Newstalgia in Product Development: Packaging and campaign updates draw on legacy equities, but add a twist to reconnect lapsed buyers or attract new demographics, with measurable impact noted in CPG and casual dining.
  • B2B Buyer Evolution: Decision-makers are demanding flexible, data-driven, and outcomes-focused solutions, increasingly influenced by wellness, ESG, and productivity considerations.
